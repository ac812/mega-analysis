adCluster - Clusters individuals and populations. For printing and analysis.
            The population cluster is suboptimal (use manualReorder)
            Produces a reorder file (to be used for plotting and analysis)

manualReorder - Takes a population list and reorders individuals according
            to the desired order.
            Produces a reorder file.
